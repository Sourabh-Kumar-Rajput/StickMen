How to play

  • Aim: Press and hold anywhere, then drag backward — like pulling a bowstring.
  • Charge: Keep holding to charge the shot — the bow flexes and the arrow glows. A fuller charge hits much harder.
  • Power: The further you pull, the harder the shot flies. Release to loose.
  • Armory: Spend coins (earned from kills) on stronger bows and elemental arrows — fire, ice, poison, air, bomb, black hole.
  • Switch ammo: Tap the bottom bar (or number keys). Tap 🛡 (or Q) to raise your shield.
  • Headshots are instant kills. Mind gravity — arrows arc, so lead moving targets.
  • Beware the boss: watch its telegraphed charge, volley, and summon attacks.
